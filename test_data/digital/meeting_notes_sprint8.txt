3. DESIGN REVIEW — SEAT TRACK ASSEMBLY REDESIGN

Raj Patel presented the redesigned seat track assembly. Key points:

Current Design:
- Material: Cold-rolled steel (CR1020)
- Weight: 2.8 kg per unit
- Cost: $15.50 per unit
- Carbon footprint: 3.25 kg CO2e per unit

Proposed Design (Option A — HSLA Steel):
- Material: HSLA steel (HSLA340)
- Weight: 2.38 kg per unit (15% reduction)
- Estimated cost: $17.50 per unit (13% increase)
- Carbon footprint: 2.80 kg CO2e per unit (14% reduction)

Proposed Design (Option B — Aluminum Alloy):
- Material: 6061-T6 Aluminum
- Weight: 1.54 kg per unit (45% reduction)
- Estimated cost: $27.50 per unit (77% increase)
- Carbon footprint: 4.10 kg CO2e per unit (26% INCREASE)

Decision: Committee approved Option A (HSLA steel) for production validation. Option B rejected due to higher carbon footprint despite weight savings — misaligned with SBTi targets. Sarah Chen noted the cost increase is offset by the $3.2M savings from broader material substitution program.
